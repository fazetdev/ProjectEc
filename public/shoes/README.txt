HOW TO ADD SHOE IMAGES:

1. Take photos of your shoes with your phone
2. Copy the image files to this folder (public/shoes/)
3. Name them descriptively:
   - blue-rubber-shoes.jpg
   - red-sneakers-women.jpg  
   - black-formal-men.jpg
4. When adding products in the app, select the filename

The app will automatically display images from this folder.
